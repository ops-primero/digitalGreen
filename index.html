FarmerChat · AI-Powered Advisory
Hyperlocal farming guidance, powered by AI

Launched in 2024, FarmerChat is an open-source AI solution delivering climate-smart, localized advice to farmers in their own language — via WhatsApp, voice, or app. Millions of queries answered, one farmer at a time.

🌾
Hyperlocal crop advice Tailored recommendations based on local soil, weather, and season
🌍
26 regional languages Farmers ask in their mother tongue, get answers they understand
🤖
Open-source & government-ready Built to strengthen public extension systems, not replace them
♀
45% women users Designed with inclusion at its core — accessibility first
