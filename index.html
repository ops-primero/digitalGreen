FarmerChat · AI-Powered Advisory
Hyperlocal farming guidance, powered by AI

Launched in 2024, FarmerChat is an open-source AI solution delivering climate-smart, localized advice to farmers in their own language — via WhatsApp, voice, or app. Millions of queries answered, one farmer at a time.

🌾
Hyperlocal crop advice Tailored recommendations based on local soil, weather, and season
🌍
26 regional languages Farmers ask in their mother tongue, get answers they understand
🤖
Open-source & government-ready Built to strengthen public extension systems, not replace them
♀
45% women users Designed with inclusion at its core — accessibility first

Our Story
From Microsoft Research to millions of farms

Digital Green began with a simple but powerful insight: farmers trust other farmers. Video-based peer learning transformed agricultural advisory in India — and that foundation now powers an AI-enabled future.

Read Our Full Story
2008
Founded at Microsoft Research Video-based agricultural advisory program begins in rural India
2012
Government partnerships scale up Collaboration with state governments across India
2016
Digital tools for frontline workers Mobile platforms for 100,000+ agricultural extension agents
2020
AI integration begins Data-driven insights woven into advisory systems
2024
FarmerChat launches Open-source AI solution reaches 600,000+ farmers, 45% women
