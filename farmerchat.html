An intelligent assistant for field-level decisions

FarmerChat is designed to support farmers with timely, local, and practical advice that makes a measurable difference in every season.

  • Grow more with optimised crop management guidance
  • Reduce risk through early pest and disease detection
  • Increase income by acting on market and price alerts
  • Adapt to changing weather and climate conditions
Available in multiple languages and built for low-connectivity environments — so no farmer is left behind, regardless of location or device.
