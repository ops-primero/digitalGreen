About Digital Green

Where technology meets community

We work at the intersection of technology, agriculture, and community development — building digital systems that help farmers access the knowledge they need, when they need it most.

Our work focuses on strengthening digital extension systems, with a strong emphasis on gender intentionality and reaching underserved farming communities across India.

From video-based learning in remote villages to AI-powered advisory on mobile phones, we have evolved continuously to meet farmers where they are.

What We Do

Connecting farmers to timely, localised agricultural advice

We design and scale digital solutions that connect farmers to practical, localised, and timely agricultural guidance — delivered through the channels they already use.

🤖

Build AI-Powered Advisory Tools

Tools like FarmerChat use large language models to deliver real-time, personalised crop guidance to millions of farmers directly on their mobile phones.

🏛️

Strengthen Extension Systems

We work with public and community-based extension systems to digitise and scale last-mile agricultural advisory, improving reach and quality of information.

📱

Enable Farmers & Frontline Workers

We equip farmer groups and frontline extension workers with digital tools, making it easier to provide timely, relevant advice at scale.

🤝

Partner to Scale Impact

We collaborate with governments, NGOs, and development organisations to deploy and scale our solutions across states and geographies.

Our Journey

From research initiative to AI-powered platform

Evolving how farmers access knowledge over the last two decades.

2006
Started at Microsoft Research

Began as a research project exploring how technology could improve agricultural extension systems, under the umbrella of Microsoft Research India.

●
2008
Scaled through NGO Partnerships

Expanded on-ground by working with NGOs to deliver video-based agricultural advisory to rural communities, reaching farmers who lacked access to traditional extension services.

●
2012
Reached 12,000+ Villages

Impacted over 100,000 farmers through localised video-based learning and advisory models, establishing a community-first approach to agricultural knowledge sharing.

●
2016
Crossed 1M Farmers Across 9 States

Scaled operations across India, strengthening reach through community-led extension networks and deepening partnerships with state governments and development organisations.

●
2022
Began AI-Driven Experimentation

Piloted chatbot-based advisory solutions in states like Jharkhand and Odisha, testing how conversational AI could provide personalised, localised crop guidance to smallholder farmers.

●
2024
Launched FarmerChat

Introduced AI-powered advisory on mobile platforms, making real-time guidance accessible to farmers in their own language — a significant shift towards scalable, digital-first agricultural advisory.

●
2025
600K+ Users on FarmerChat

Rapid adoption across India marks a decisive shift towards scalable, digital-first agricultural advisory — with farmers actively using AI tools to make better decisions on their farms.

●

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌾

Farmer-first

Everything we build is grounded in real farmer needs. Our solutions are designed from the field up — not the boardroom down.

♀

Inclusion by design

Prioritising women and underserved communities is not an afterthought — it is built into our product design, partnerships, and measurement.

🔗

Collaboration

We work with partners across ecosystems — governments, research institutions, NGOs, and the private sector — because lasting impact requires collective action.

💡

Innovation with purpose

We use technology to solve real problems. Every innovation is evaluated against the question: does this make a meaningful difference to a farmer's life?
